About Us

Our Mission

Re:Discover aims to spread awareness of neocolonialism and its effects among the Filipino youth through the use of easily accessible content that can inspire Filipino adolescents to take pride in Philippine culture.

Our Vision

Through Re:Discover, we will be able to create a generation of Filipinos who fully embrace and preserve their own cultural identity.

Rationale

Even though the Philippines has a lot of rich cultural heritage, neocolonialism continues to affect many Filipinos, especially the youth, in terms of their values, preferences, and aspirations as they continuously get exposed to foreign media, weakening cultural identity. With Re:Discover, Filipino adolescents can effectively learn about the effects of neocolonialism and creates a space for them to explore and rediscover the Philippine culture.
